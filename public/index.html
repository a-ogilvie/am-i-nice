Welcome! Click on the button to send the newest posts (up to 200) to be analysed for their sentiment, and discover if your posts are more positive or negative. The most positive and negative posts will also be displayed.

This data is not captured or stored anywhere. Only the text content of the posts is sent to Google for analysis. Personally identifiable information does not leave your own computer.
